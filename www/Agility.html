Agility is a measure of your overall fitness and reflexes. It affects the number of action points in V.A.T.S. and your ability to sneak.

Gunslinger

More damage with pistols. Gunslinger has ranks that up the range of pistols, add a chance to disarm enemies, and even instant limb crippling shots

  • Rigorous combat training means Pistols do 20% more damage
  • Attacks with Pistols do 40% more damage
  • Attacks with Pistols do 60% more damage
  • Attacks with Pistols do 80% more damage
  • Attacks with Pistols do 100% more damage

Commando

More damage done by Automatic weapons

  • Rigorous combat training means automatic weapons do 20% more damage
  • Attacks with automatic weapons do 40% more damage
  • Attacks with automatic weapons do 60% more damage
  • Attacks with automatic weapons do 80% more damage
  • Attacks with automatic weapons do 100% more damage

Sneak

Become harder to detect when in sneak mode

  • Become whisper, become shadow. You are 20% harder to detect while sneaking.
  • You are 40% harder to detect while sneaking
  • You are 60% harder to detect while sneaking
  • You are 80% harder to detect while sneaking
  • You are 100% harder to detect while sneaking

Mister Sandman

Kill humans or humanoid creatures in their sleep for bonus XP.

  • Sneaking up on a sleeping human will give you the option to silently kill them for double XP
  • Sneaking up on a sleeping human or ghoul will give you the option to silently kill them for triple XP
  • Sneaking up on a sleeping human, ghoul, or super mutant will give you the option to silently kill them for quadruple XP
