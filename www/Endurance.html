Cannibal

Your strong stomach allows you to feed from human or humanoid corpses in order to regain health.

  • Feeding on a human corpse will enable you to restore part of your health
  • Feeding on a feral or non-feral ghoul corpse will enable you to restore part of your health
  • Feeding on a super mutant corpse will enable you to restore part of your health
  • Flesh can now be taken from human and humanoid corpses, which can be eaten at a later time to restore health
