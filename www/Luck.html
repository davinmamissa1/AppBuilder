Mysterious Stranger

The Mysterious Stranger will sometimes come to your aid when in V.A.T.S.

  • There is a 10% chance that the Mysterious Stranger will appear during V.A.T.S. combat wielding a silenced pistol
  • There is a 15% chance that the Mysterious Stranger will appear during V.A.T.S. combat wielding a hunting rifle
  • There is a 20% chance that the Mysterious Stranger will appear during V.A.T.S. combat wielding a tommy gun
  • There is a 30% chance that the Mysterious Stranger will accompany you for a short while after V.A.T.S. combat. The Stranger will engage in dialogue and can be given commands
