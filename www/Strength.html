Strength is a measure of raw physical power. It affects how much you can carry, and the damage of all melee attacks

Iron Fist

Increased damage with fists and unarmed weapons.

  • 20% increase of Unarmed Damage
  • Do 40% more unarmed damage, with a chance to cripple limbs
  • Do 60% more unarmed damage, with a chance to stagger your opponent following a blow to the torso
  • Do 80% more unarmed damage, with a chance to knock down your opponent following a blow to the head
  • Do 100% more unarmed damage, with a chance to paralyze your opponent in V.A.T.S. (level 46 required)

Big Leagues

Increased damage with melee weapons.

  • Swing for the fences! Do 20% more melee weapon damage
  • Do 40% more melee weapon damage, with a chance to cripple limbs
  • Do 60% more melee weapon damage, with a chance to stagger your opponent following a blow to the torso
  • Do 80% more melee weapon damage, with a chance to knock down your opponent following a blow to the head
  • Do 100% more melee weapon damage, with a chance to instant kill your opponent following a blow to the head

Armorer

Create, Modify and repair armor

  • You gain access to base level and Rank 1 armor mods
  • You gain access to Rank 2 armor mods
  • You gain access to Rank 3 armor mods
  • You gain access to Rank 4 armor mods

Weaponsmith

Modify and repair melee weapons

  • You gain access to base level and Rank 1 melee weapon mods
  • You gain access to Rank 2 melee weapon mods
  • You gain access to Rank 3 melee weapon mods
  • You gain access to Rank 4 melee weapon mods

Big Guns

Increased damage with heavy weapons.

  • Attacks with heavy weapons do 20% more damage
  • Attacks with heavy weapons do 40% more damage
  • Attacks with heavy weapons do 60% more damage
  • Attacks with heavy weapons do 80% more damage
  • Attacks with heavy weapons do 100% more damage

Strong Back

Carry more items and improve your mobility when over-encumbered

  • You are now able to carry an additional 50 lbs of equipment
  • You are now able to carry an additional 100 lbs of equipment, and can run whilst over-encumbered
  • You are now able to carry an additional 200 lbs of equipment, and can fast travel whilst over-encumbered

Size Matters

Reduced recoil from all ranged weapons

  • Recoil from ranged weapons reduced by 20%
  • Recoil from ranged weapons reduced by 40%
  • Recoil from ranged weapons reduced by 60%
  • Recoil from ranged weapons reduced by 80%

Defender

Increased damage when using the melee attack of your pistol or rifle

  • Pistol and rifle melee attacks do 20% more damage
  • Pistol and rifle melee attacks do 40% more damage
  • Pistol and rifle melee attacks do 60% more damage
  • Pistol and rifle melee attacks do 80% more damage
  • Pistol and rifle melee attacks do 100% more damage

Slayer

Bonus damage and less chance of being staggered or knocked down when wielding two-handed melee weapons.

  • Wielding a two-handed melee weapon grants you 50% bonus damage, and halves your chance of being staggered or knocked down
  • Wielding a two-handed melee weapon grants you 75% bonus damage, and prevents you from being staggered
  • Wielding a two-handed melee weapon grants you 100% bonus damage, and prevents you from being knocked down

Juggernaut

Charge into your enemies when sprinting in order to knock them down.

  • Enemies in your path will be knocked down when you charge into them
  • Enemies in your path will be knocked down when you charge into them, and will receive damage
  • Enemies in your path will be knocked down when you charge into them, and will receive critical damage with a chance of being killed by the blow
